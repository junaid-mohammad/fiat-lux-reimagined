Community Consultation

The Fiat Lux Feasibility Study and Master Plan published in 2015 and related initiatives are dependent upon a high level of community involvement and collaboration to ensure the project reflects the needs and aspirations of all who work in the Library. A series of facilitated communication methods have been implemented to gather input and inspiration.

Current Consultations

  • Governance committee
  • Advisory functional planning groups: User & Academic Services, Rare & Special Collections, General Collections, and Staffing Needs

Upcoming Consultations

  • Governance and working committees
  • Advisory functional planning groups: User & Academic Services, Rare & Special Collections, General Collections, and Staffing Needs
